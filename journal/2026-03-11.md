### Annotated List of Data Sources from Conversation

- **U.S. Wind Turbine Database (USWTDB)**: [https://energy.usgs.gov/uswtdb](https://energy.usgs.gov/uswtdb)  
  Annotation: Maintained by USGS, LBNL, and ACP; contains 70,000+ turbine records with operational details like locations, commissioning dates, specs, and project performance; updated quarterly; used for actual installed capacities and operational metrics.

- **USWTDB Viewer**: [https://www.usgs.gov/tools/us-wind-turbine-database-uswtdb-viewer](https://www.usgs.gov/tools/us-wind-turbine-database-uswtdb-viewer)  
  Annotation: Interactive web tool for visualizing, inspecting, and downloading USWTDB data; includes filters for operational status and technical specs; supports analysis of real turbine performance.

- **United States Wind Turbine Database (ver. 8.2, December 2025)**: [https://data.usgs.gov/datacatalog/data/USGS:6001e327d34e592d8671fae0](https://data.usgs.gov/datacatalog/data/USGS:6001e327d34e592d8671fae0)  
  Annotation: USGS data release with turbine locations and specs from 1982-2025; downloadable dataset for operational analysis.

- **NREL Wind Data and Tools**: [https://www.nlr.gov/wind/data-tools](https://www.nlr.gov/wind/data-tools)  
  Annotation: Includes Annual Technology Baseline with real-world cost and performance data; Cambium datasets with hourly metrics; meteorological data from turbine sites; used for capacity factors and generation calculations.

- **Wind Resource Database (WRDB)**: [https://wrdb.nrel.gov/](https://wrdb.nrel.gov/)  
  Annotation: NREL public interface for wind resource datasets; includes high-resolution operational data for U.S. and international sites.

- **Wind Toolkit Data Downloads API**: [https://developer.nrel.gov/docs/wind/wind-toolkit](https://developer.nrel.gov/docs/wind/wind-toolkit)  
  Annotation: NREL API for downloading WIND Toolkit data; includes meteorological and turbine power for 126,000+ U.S. sites (2007-2013); supports capacity factor calculations.

- **New Wind Resource Database (NREL)**: [https://www.energy.gov/eere/wind/articles/new-wind-resource-database-includes-updated-wind-toolkit](https://www.energy.gov/eere/wind/articles/new-wind-resource-database-includes-updated-wind-toolkit)  
  Annotation: Updated WIND Toolkit with 5-min, 2-km resolution data; over 1 million GB of wind resource metrics for performance analysis.

- **U.S. Data - WRDB (NREL)**: [https://wrdb.nrel.gov/data-sets/us-data](https://wrdb.nrel.gov/data-sets/us-data)  
  Annotation: Specific U.S. datasets from WRDB; includes WIND Toolkit and WTK-LED variants at 5-min and 60-min resolutions for capacity and generation.

- **The Wind Power Database**: [https://www.thewindpower.net/index_en.php](https://www.thewindpower.net/index_en.php)  
  Annotation: Global database with 32,065 wind farms and 2,053 turbine models; operational data on capacities, hub heights, commissioning; exportable in Excel/CSV; used for installed MW/GW and performance.

- **Global Wind Power Tracker**: [https://globalenergymonitor.org/projects/global-wind-power-tracker](https://globalenergymonitor.org/projects/global-wind-power-tracker)  
  Annotation: GEM dataset with 32,227 utility-scale wind farm phases (10 MW+); operating capacities (1,129 GW), statuses; facility-specific operational details.

- **Global Wind Power Tracker - Overview**: [https://www.arcgis.com/home/item.html?id=58b9ec71728c40f3b8a6f37bacf0124e](https://www.arcgis.com/home/item.html?id=58b9ec71728c40f3b8a6f37bacf0124e)  
  Annotation: ArcGIS layer for GWPT locations; based on June 2024 update.

- **Global Wind Power Tracker Methodology**: [https://www.gem.wiki/Global_Wind_Power_Tracker_Methodology](https://www.gem.wiki/Global_Wind_Power_Tracker_Methodology)  
  Annotation: Details on GWPT organization; unit-level data for operational tracking.

- **SCADA Dataset for Wind Turbine Operation and Maintenance**: [https://www.nature.com/articles/s41597-024-03067-9](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41597-024-03067-9)  
  Annotation: Scientific Data publication; 3-year (2014-2017) dataset from five 2.5 MW Fuhrländer turbines in Spain; 312 variables from 78 sensors at 5-min intervals; includes min/max/mean/std dev for subsystems; alarm events.

- **Wind Turbine Scada Dataset**: [https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/berkerisen/wind-turbine-scada-dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/berkerisen/wind-turbine-scada-dataset)  
  Annotation: Kaggle dataset; 10-min SCADA intervals for wind speed, direction, power; used for operational analysis.

- **SCADA system dataset exploration**: [https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2590123022003103](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2590123022003103)  
  Annotation: Time-series dataset for wind power prediction; includes cleaning and ML-based forecasting.

- **Dataset on SCADA Data of an Urban Small Wind Turbine**: [https://www.mdpi.com/2306-5729/8/3/52](https://www.mdpi.com/2306-5729/8/3/52)  
  Annotation: MDPI dataset; 2017-2022 data from 1.8 kW turbine; electrical/mechanical quantities, voltages, currents, power; capacity factor calculations.

- **SCADA data for wind turbine data-driven condition monitoring**: [https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/0309524X221124031](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/0309524X221124031)  
  Annotation: Review of SCADA-based models for O&M; classification/regression for failures and performance.

- **IEA Wind Task 43 Open Data**: [https://iea-wind.org/task43/task-43-open-data](https://iea-wind.org/task43/task-43-open-data)  
  Annotation: Curated repository; includes Danish monthly production since 2002, Elia Belgium 15-min data since 2017, ENGIE La Haute Borne 10-min data (2013-2020), NREL integration datasets (2004-2013).

- **Task 43 – Wind Energy Digitalization**: [https://iea-wind.org/task43](https://iea-wind.org/task43)  
  Annotation: Overview of Task 43; focuses on digital tools for operational data.

- **Task 43 Publications**: [https://iea-wind.org/task43/t43-publications](https://iea-wind.org/task43/t43-publications)  
  Annotation: Reports on data standards and open datasets for wind energy.

- **IEA Wind Digitalization (Task 43)**: [https://github.com/IEA-Task-43](https://github.com/IEA-Task-43)  
  Annotation: GitHub repo; pre-construction energy estimate standards; open-source resources.

- **International Energy Agency Wind Task 43 Digitalization: Data Standards Gap Analysis**: [https://zenodo.org/records/13225353](https://zenodo.org/records/13225353)  
  Annotation: Analysis of industry standards for wind data.

- **PNNL Distributed Wind Project Database**: [https://www.pnnl.gov/distributed-wind/market-report/data](https://www.pnnl.gov/distributed-wind/market-report/data)  
  Annotation: Dataset with generation, cost, operational data for U.S. distributed wind (<30 MW); includes annual output and incentives; searchable by location, year, capacity.

- **Distributed Wind Project Database (OSTI)**: [https://www.osti.gov/biblio/1959185](https://www.osti.gov/biblio/1959185)  
  Annotation: Detailed collection from manufacturers and stakeholders; cost, incentive, generation data.

- **Distributed Wind | PNNL**: [https://www.pnnl.gov/distributed-wind](https://www.pnnl.gov/distributed-wind)  
  Annotation: Overview; market reports with operational data.

- **Distributed Wind Market Report**: [https://www.pnnl.gov/distributed-wind/technology-update](https://www.pnnl.gov/distributed-wind/technology-update)  
  Annotation: Annual updates; 2025 technology data; links to full dataset.

- **Open-Source Wind Power Datasets Review 2022 Scientific Data**: [https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1002/we.2766](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1002/we.2766)

- **Towards high resolution, validated and open global wind power assessments**: [https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-026-68337-z](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-026-68337-z)

- **A collection and categorization of open‐source wind and wind power datasets**: [https://docs.wind-watch.org/open%E2%80%90source-wind-power-datasets.pdf](https://docs.wind-watch.org/open%E2%80%90source-wind-power-datasets.pdf)

- **ENGIE La Haute Borne Wind Farm Dataset**: [https://opendata-renewables.engie.com/explore/dataset/d543716b-368d-4c53-8fb1-55addbe8d3ad/information](https://opendata-renewables.engie.com/explore/dataset/d543716b-368d-4c53-8fb1-55addbe8d3ad/information)
