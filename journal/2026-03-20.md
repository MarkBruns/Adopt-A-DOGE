# Building Community Emergency Response Preparedness Committees (CERPC)

This list of 20 areas revisits and expands upon our discussion that started with [INTRODUCING the idea of Community Emergency Response Preparedness Committees (CERPC)](https://github.com/MarkBruns/Adopt-A-DOGE/blob/main/journal/2026-03-12.md).

**1. Fundamental Starting Area: Community Recruitment, Engagement, and Kingdom-Building Coordination Hub**  
- Faith-driven recruitment through Lord’s Prayer gatherings framing preparedness as Kingdom obedience  
- Ishikawa fishbone assessments mapping member gifts to all 20 tactical areas  
- Agentic Discovery Build Days and rotating mini-workshops for skill sampling  
- Fast-track progression: participant → recruiter → trainer → content developer → entrepreneur  
- Multi-agent Kingdom Crews using ReAct loops, swarm brainstorming, critique, and dogfooding  
- Launch self-funded micro-businesses employing high-agency citizens across all areas  
- Shared digital workspace for progress tracking, content iteration, and integration  
- Goal: Build an autodidactic learning community of trainers, developers, and resilient employers who manifest God’s Kingdom on earth

**2. Electrician**  
- Install/maintain generators, battery storage systems, inverters, and transfer switches for homes and critical facilities  
- Conduct home/community energy audits and resilient backup power system design  
- Teach safe emergency wiring, grounding, load calculation, and blackout resilience techniques  
- Develop AR/VR training modules, certification workshops, and open-source guides  
- Form local generator and backup power installation cooperatives  
- Create businesses employing community members in resilient electrical solutions  
- Kingdom tie-in: Steward reliable power as practical provision and light for neighbors

**3. Plumbing**  
- Implement rainwater harvesting, greywater recycling, and emergency purification  
- Train DIY repairs, leak detection, and large-scale water storage planning  
- Organize cistern builds, bulk storage co-ops, and community distribution networks  
- Produce preservation and purification educational content  
- Launch water-system retrofit and consulting micro-businesses  
- Kingdom focus: Ensure reliable “living water” access during scarcity

**4. Carpentry**  
- Reinforce homes against storms and build modular shelters/storage  
- Host community build-days for sheds, raised beds, and emergency barriers  
- Teach sustainable materials and quick-assembly modular techniques  
- Create AR/VR build simulations and construction guides  
- Establish carpentry cooperatives offering preparedness retrofits  
- Kingdom expression: Build physical refuge as tangible expression of God’s shelter

**5. Masonry**  
- Construct flood barriers, retaining walls, and sealed foundations  
- Train basic concrete work, erosion control, and weather-resistant repairs  
- Organize preemptive infrastructure hardening projects  
- Develop visual guides and community tool libraries  
- Form businesses in mitigation and repair services  
- Kingdom tie-in: Build on the rock (Matthew 7) against literal and spiritual storms

**6. Medical (Preventive & Chronic Care)**  
- Deliver first aid certification, vaccination drives, and chronic illness protocols  
- Stockpile and manage community medical kits and caches  
- Run health fairs and pandemic/isolation response planning  
- Create AR/VR medical training modules and neighborhood plans  
- Launch local wellness co-ops and medical supply businesses  
- Kingdom focus: Heal the sick and care for the vulnerable as Jesus modeled

**7. Welding/Machining**  
- Fabricate security gates, repair racks, mobile carts, and custom tools  
- Teach safe workshop practices and community tool library management  
- Run custom fabrication workshops using local materials  
- Produce blueprints and safety training content  
- Build businesses in preparedness gear and equipment repairs  
- Kingdom expression: Shape tools that serve and protect the community

**8. Fabrication / Additive Manufacturing**  
- Prototype containers, adaptive devices, and reusable gear  
- Lead maker-spaces focused on upcycling and resource efficiency  
- Train manual and digital fabrication techniques  
- Develop shared print files and crafting curricula  
- Establish fabrication co-ops and upcycling enterprises  
- Kingdom culture: Turn scarcity into abundance through creative dominion

**9. Intel & Information Pulling**  
- Map local hazards, monitor weather, and track supply chains  
- Train data collection, early-warning systems, and fact-checking  
- Teach “pull information” skills versus propaganda consumption  
- Create risk assessment tools and community intelligence networks  
- Form local intel co-ops and verified news services  
- Kingdom impact: Walk in truth and wisdom as light in darkness

**10. Gardening, Nutrition & Preventive Medicine**  
- Build community gardens and teach canning/dehydration methods  
- Design nutrition plans using local, storable foods  
- Train hygiene protocols and balanced emergency diets  
- Produce seed banks, AR garden planners, and preservation guides  
- Launch food security co-ops and gardening supply businesses  
- Kingdom parallel: Sow and reap as both practical and spiritual principle

**11. Landscaping, Arborism & Tree Management**  
- Conduct hazard tree assessments and safe pruning/removal  
- Create firebreaks, windbreaks, and erosion control landscapes  
- Train safe chainsaw and arborist techniques  
- Develop AR hazard identification tools and assessment checklists  
- Form mitigation service businesses and community crews  
- Kingdom stewardship: Tend creation for protection and beauty

**12. Animal Husbandry & Meat Processing**  
- Teach ethical backyard livestock raising (chickens, rabbits)  
- Train coop building, humane processing, and smoking techniques  
- Build community livestock sharing networks  
- Create husbandry manuals and AR training modules  
- Establish protein co-ops and processing micro-enterprises  
- Kingdom dominion: Responsible care for animals as food provision

**13. Field Food Storage & Fermentation**  
- Design root cellars, vacuum sealing, and dehydration systems  
- Run bulk-buy co-ops and household storage rotation audits  
- Teach long-term preservation against pests and spoilage  
- Develop AR storage simulators and preservation guides  
- Launch consulting businesses in emergency food security  
- Kingdom principle: Store wisely for future provision

**14. Plant Identification & Foraging**  
- Lead community foraging walks and edible/medicinal plant identification  
- Build seed banks and biodiversity planning  
- Integrate foraging into emergency food strategies  
- Produce AR identification guides and local species databases  
- Form foraging co-ops and seed-saving businesses  
- Kingdom wonder: Recognize God’s provision in the wild

**15. Hunting, Trapping, Fishing & Wildlife Management**  
- Teach legal/ethical harvesting and conservation practices  
- Run licensing courses and gear-sharing programs  
- Plan sustainable supplemental protein strategies  
- Create AR field guides and scenario training  
- Build management service businesses  
- Kingdom expression: Wise dominion over wildlife resources

**16. HVAC Installation & Maintenance**  
- Audit and retrofit insulation, passive cooling, and ventilation  
- Train energy-efficient systems for extreme weather resilience  
- Lead home energy efficiency and retrofitting workshops  
- Develop AR/VR HVAC training modules  
- Establish efficiency consulting and installation businesses  
- Kingdom goal: Create healthy living spaces for human flourishing

**17. Tool & Equipment Maintenance (Knife-making, Armorer, Drones)**  
- Maintain generators, radios, vehicles, and community equipment  
- Teach advanced repair skills including FPV drones and legal gunsmithing  
- Build durability testing and maintenance protocols  
- Produce repair manuals and AR technique guides  
- Launch equipment service and customization businesses  
- Kingdom culture: Keep tools ready for those who serve

**18. Communications & Ham Radio Networks**  
- Build neighborhood person-to-person chains and radio repeater networks  
- Certify operators and run regular communication drills  
- Design backup comms plans and antenna systems  
- Create radio manuals, AR antenna simulators, and protocols  
- Form communication service co-ops  
- Kingdom unity: Ensure every voice can be heard when systems fail

**19. Practical Physical Fitness & Martial Arts (Jesus & Jiu-Jitsu Model)**  
- Attend elite Jiu-Jitsu seminars worldwide and translate into free community events  
- Build functional fitness for emergency strength, endurance, grip, and mental toughness  
- Run scenario-based training (injury carries, prolonged labor, stress inoculation)  
- Develop discipleship curriculum integrating Scripture, discipline, and fruit-bearing  
- Produce AR/VR conditioning programs and testimony content  
- Establish Christian martial arts academies as mission outposts and preparedness hubs  
- Curated resource: [BJJ-notebook](https://github.com/ajharris/BJJ-notebook)  
- Kingdom impact: Reach the unchurched through gyms and forge strong disciples who represent Christ

**20. Agentic Autodidactic Integration, Continuous Mastery & Train-the-Trainer Flywheel (Overarching All Areas)**  
- Continuously scan, curate, and integrate world-class seminars, train-the-trainer programs, and open resources for every tactical area  
- Master and apply the 26 Major Agentic Patterns (ReAct loops, multi-agent swarms + voting, hierarchical decomposition, review-and-critique, blackboard shared workspace, reflection, persistent memory, role-based crews, graph-of-thoughts, agentic RAG + meta-learning, etc.) from the Adopt-A-DOGE journal  
- Dogfood all patterns in community blackboard workspace to eliminate D.O.W.N.T.I.M.E. wastes and accelerate skill mastery  
- Run ongoing ReAct + reflection loops and multi-agent crews to update awareness of global best practices  
- Develop and iterate AR/VR trench-story curricula using swarm brainstorming, critique, and persistent memory patterns  
- Turn every participant into trainer/learner/developer/entrepreneur through handoff, router, and evolutionary adaptation patterns  
- Launch agentic learning co-ops and micro-businesses that teach patterns community-wide  
- Curated resources: [Awesome Agentic Patterns](https://github.com/nibzard/awesome-agentic-patterns) • [Awesome AI Agents](https://github.com/kyrolabs/awesome-agents)  
- Kingdom expression: Learn any “language” of preparedness together with a community of masters—living out the Lord’s Prayer through high-agency, self-improving discipleship that bears eternal fruit

By embedding these 20 areas within the agentic, autodidactic framework, CERPCs create a living, ever-learning demonstration of God’s Kingdom on earth: resilient, physically strong, intellectually sharp, and unstoppable. Preparation itself becomes the deepest form of worship, fellowship, and mission.
